Pricing FAQ

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers.

Are there any long-term contracts?

Our Starter and Professional plans are one-time projects. We offer optional monthly retainers for ongoing support and maintenance. Enterprise plans are typically structured as a 6 or 12-month engagement.

What does 'custom' mean in the Enterprise plan?

It means we work with you to design a completely bespoke AI automation strategy. This can include automating complex internal workflows, building custom AI tools for your team, or developing a multi-channel AI presence (web, phone, social media). The scope and price are tailored to your specific goals.
