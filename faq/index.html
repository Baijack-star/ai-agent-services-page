What is an AI chatbot?

An AI chatbot is a software application designed to simulate human conversation through text or voice. It can answer customer questions, provide information, and automate tasks 24/7, improving customer service and operational efficiency.

What is an AI voice agent?

An AI voice agent is like a chatbot but for phone calls. It can handle inbound and outbound calls, answer questions, book appointments, and route calls to the appropriate human agent, all with a natural-sounding voice.

How does workflow automation work?

Workflow automation connects different software applications to create automated sequences of tasks. For example, when a new lead fills out a form on your website, the AI can automatically add them to your CRM, send a welcome email, and schedule a follow-up task for your sales team.
